The beach is beautiful with soft white sands but suffers from a high quantity of seaweed. The hotel is right on the beach and close to a coral reef ideal for divers. There are also two sheltered shingle beaches and a small tidal island which can be climbedThe beach appears to be alive and joyful with the presence of the natural vegetation. There are evergreen plantations both along and inside the beach. Images of buoyant seaweeds can be seen along the shore.
